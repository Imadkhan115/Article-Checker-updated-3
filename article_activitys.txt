ARTICLE USAGE ACTIVITY (A / AN / THE)

PART A — Fill in the Blanks
Insert a, an, or the.

1. I saw ___ elephant at the zoo yesterday.
2. She bought ___ umbrella because it was raining.
3. He is ___ honest man.
4. They live in ___ old house on the hill.
5. I need ___ hour to finish this.
6. She wants to be ___ engineer.
7. He found ___ one-dollar coin on the ground.
8. We visited ___ university near our town.
9. Can you pass me ___ apple from the basket?
10. She is reading ___ interesting book.
11. My sister adopted ___ cat from the shelter.
12. She cooked ___ meal for ___ guests.
13. He is watching ___ movie we talked about.
14. They bought ___ new laptop yesterday.
15. She has ___ idea that might help.

Each sentence has one incorrect article.

PART C — Choose the Correct Article
Circle the correct option.

1. He bought (a / an) uniform.
2. She saw (a / an) owl in the tree.
3. They need (a / the) permission to enter.
4. I found (a / an) USB cable.
5. She had (a / an) idea for the project.
6. He is (a / an) heir to the fortune.
7. We went to (a / the) beach yesterday.
8. She read (a / the) book you recommended.
9. He wants to buy (a / an) motorcycle.
10. She found (a / an) unusual stone.
